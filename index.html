01 — About

A builder at the intersection of code, security, and people.

I am a Computer Science undergraduate at The Catholic University of America (minor in Mathematics) drawn to the places where clean engineering meets real-world trust — full-stack apps that small businesses actually use, and security tooling that makes complex systems a little less opaque.

My work spans Django backends and React front ends, automation with Ansible, and cybersecurity fundamentals picked up at MITRE and sharpened in coursework on network security and operating systems. I care about shipping clearly, documenting thoroughly, and building with the person on the other side of the screen in mind.

Beyond code, I enjoy helping others and the community. It was people who share that passion that have helped push and shape me to be where I am at now. I believe in the power of community and shared knowledge to lift everyone up.

This semester I'm studying abroad at the University of Galway in Ireland — writing code in a new timezone and learning how engineers think on the other side of the Atlantic.
